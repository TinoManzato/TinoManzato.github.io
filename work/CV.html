Further details of the current activity


Forecasting with sounding-derived indices and Artificial Neural Network:

I have used the sounding-derived indices as input of ANN ensemble for short-range forecast of thunderstorm and rain occurrence and intensity. Recently I have applied the same methodology to ECMWF model-derived pseudo-indices (work under submission) and also to the hail problem.

Downscaling:

I have supervised a mathematic student during his thesis on downscaling of maximum and minimum daily temperatures from ECMWF data using a linear multiregression (MOS). It is planned to expand this work to more stations and also to non-linear techniques.

Forecast Verification:

I'm particularly interested in verification of the binary (or multi-categorical) classifiers using the Signal Detection Theory and in particular the maximum Pierce Skill Score. I'm also interested in combining classifiers in ensemble (bagging, boosting, MV,...).
